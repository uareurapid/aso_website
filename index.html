Questions

Frequently asked

Does the free plan need a credit card?

+

No. The free plan is completely free forever — no credit card, no trial period. You get 1 app, 10 keywords, and weekly refreshes with no strings attached.

What App Store Connect permissions do I need?

+

You'll need an App Store Connect API key with "Sales and Reports" access to fetch performance metrics (downloads, impressions, page views). Keyword rank tracking works without API credentials for basic use; connecting ASC unlocks the full analytics dashboard.

How often are keyword rankings updated?

+

Free plan: weekly. Starter & Pro: daily. Elite: up to 5 refreshes per day per app. You can also trigger a manual refresh from the dashboard at any time, subject to plan cooldown limits.

Can I track Android / Google Play apps?

+

Not yet. ASO Analytics currently focuses exclusively on the Apple App Store. Google Play support is on the roadmap for a future phase.

Is my App Store Connect key stored securely?

+

Yes. Your private key is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption before being stored in the database. It is never logged or transmitted in plaintext. You can revoke and replace it at any time from your settings page.

Can I cancel anytime?

+

Absolutely. All paid plans are month-to-month. Cancel directly from your account settings — your access continues until the end of the current billing period, then you automatically drop to the free plan.
